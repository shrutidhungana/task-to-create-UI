Chicken Momo:

Ingredients

  • 1/2kg minced chicken
  • 2 medium size onions, 1 would be okay if it's big
  • 600g refined flour
  • Some spring onions
  • Some cloves of ginger and garlic minced
  • 200g cabbage
  • 1/2kg refined wheat flour
  • Some pinch of corainder
  • Salt as per taste
  • 1/2 table spoon turmeric powder
  • Momo-masala
  • 1 table spoon chilly powder
  • Mustard oil

Directions

  1. Take some amount of refined wheat flour and make a dough out it to cover it for sometime.
  2. Take some minced chicken and add vegetables such as cabbage,onion, ginger garlic paste,spring onions,corainder and mix them well.
  3. Put some amount of momo-masala,salt,turmeric powder, and oil as well as some water to make them juicy.
  4. Take one half of the dough to roll it into a big circle like structureand cut it into small pieces with some round structure.
  5. Take the cut piece, add the mixture and pack it.
  6. Boil some water and put the packed uncooked momo for it to be steamed.
  7. Cook it till 15 minutes and check if it is cooked or not.
  8. The momo is cooked if it isn't sticky.

Do you wanna learn about position of chefs?

Following are the position of some chef roles:

Purchasing Manager
The purchasing manager is in charge of buying all of the food for the kitchen.
Kitchen Porter
This person is in charge of simple but important tasks involved in the basic preparations of the food.
Commis Chef
A junior chef, also called the commis chef, has recently completed some schooling or training and is beginning to work in the culinary field.
Chef de Partie
These chefs cook the food that is being served to customers.
Sous Chef
The deputy chef, otherwise known as the sous chef, remains second-in-command in the kitchen.
Chef de Cuisine
Like an executive chef, this person controls all aspects of the kitchen.
Executive Chef
Their primary role is managing the kitchen and its staff.
